About Project

We offer a variety of services regarding casting to suit your project. Along with these, we offer a complete Design of Feeder and Gating System with Complete Filling and Temperature analysis. With our Software lab and Technical persons, we can prepare sand castings for analysis to help reduce casting defects and process inefficiencies.

Our solutions also allow for the use of a wide range of materials. Our equipment includes Altair Inspire Cast software to reduce or eliminate porosity. We also utilize Solidworks 3D modeling Software for modeling of casting and Gating system.

Design Of Casting Accessories

The process in which the metal is first liquefied by properly heating it in a suitable furnace and pouring the molten metal in a previously prepared mould cavity where it is allowed to solidify is termed as metal casting.

In this project we are design Casting Accessories like Runner, Spure,Ingate etc.
